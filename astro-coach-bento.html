No. Module I is designed for complete beginners and covers everything from horoscope casting to basic interpretation. Students with prior knowledge simply move faster.

The Professional Astrologer program covers 24–28 sessions across Modules I–III. The AstroCoach program adds 36+ sessions total, the Astro Profile system, psychology training, transformation programs, supervised practice, placement support and full certification. If you want to build a professional practice and earn sustainably, choose AstroCoach.

Expect 6–10 hours per week — live sessions on weekends, recorded content on weekdays, plus practice. All live sessions are recorded and available within 24 hours.
