The Astro Coach program includes everything in the Astrologer track, plus 36+ sessions total, the Astro Profile system, psychology training, 1-month & 3-month transformation programs, supervised client practice, placement support, and full Astro Coach certification. If you want to build a professional coaching practice and earn sustainably, Astro Coach is the right choice.

Expect 6–10 hours per week — live sessions on weekends, recorded content on weekdays, and practice exercises. All live sessions are recorded and available within 24 hours.

We cannot guarantee specific earnings, but we give you everything needed to create them.
